Flight Searches for Abu Dhabi

66,851 (+26.8% YoY)

Flight searches from Japan for Abu Dhabi totaled 66,851 in 2024, showing strong growth of 26.8% compared to 2023's 52,722 searches, indicating growing travel intent.

Search Pattern Analysis:

Peak search activity occurs in September (7,467 searches) and October (7,252 searches), indicating advance planning for winter travel. The growth represents an additional 14,129 flight searches year-over-year.

Strategic Implications:

Hotel Guests for Abu Dhabi

17,001 (+97.8% YoY)

Hotel guests from Japan reached 17,001 in 2024, representing exceptional growth of 97.8% compared to 2023's 8,595 guests, establishing Japan as a premier Asia-Pacific market.
